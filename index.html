MyApp – Privacy Policy

Regox is developed and maintained by Rego. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email) when creating an account
  • Usage data to improve features and performance

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your experience
  • To improve app functionality
  • To respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion at any time (see below).

Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so from within the MyApp mobile application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Regox mobile app
  2. Navigate to Side menu, And Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm your decision
  4. Then add your current password and confirm current password
  5. Confirm your decision

Once deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
